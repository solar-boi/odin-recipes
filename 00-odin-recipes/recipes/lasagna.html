Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich tomato sauce, ground meat (often beef or sausage), ricotta or mozzarella cheese, and herbs like basil and oregano. The layers are baked until bubbly and golden, creating a hearty, comforting meal with a blend of savory flavors and textures.
