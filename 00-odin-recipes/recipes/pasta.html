Pasta

Description

Pasta is a staple of Italian cuisine made from dough that is typically shaped into noodles, then boiled and combined with a variety of sauces like marinara, Alfredo, or pesto. Pasta comes in many shapes and sizes, such as spaghetti, penne, and fettuccine, making it adaptable for different dishes and flavors.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • Tomato sauce or Alfredo sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Drain the pasta and drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Prepare the sauce of your choice (tomato or cream-based).
  4. Mix the cooked pasta with the sauce.
  5. Top with Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs.
  6. Serve with garlic bread on the side and enjoy.
