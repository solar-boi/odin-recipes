Sushi

Description

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that typically consists of vinegared rice, fresh raw fish or seafood, and vegetables, often wrapped in seaweed (nori). It can be served as rolls (maki), individual pieces (nigiri), or in other styles like sashimi. Sushi is known for its delicate flavors, presentation, and balance of textures.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Fresh raw fish or seafood (salmon, tuna, etc.)
  • Vegetables (avocado, cucumber, etc.)
  • Soy sauce and wasabi

Steps

  1. Prepare the sushi rice with vinegar.
  2. Slice the fresh fish and vegetables.
  3. Lay a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat and spread rice on top.
  4. Add fish and vegetables in the center.
  5. Roll the sushi tightly using the mat.
  6. Slice and serve with soy sauce and wasabi.
