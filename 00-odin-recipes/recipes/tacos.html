Tacos

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish made by filling soft or crispy corn or flour tortillas with a variety of ingredients such as seasoned meat, cheese, lettuce, salsa, and avocado. Tacos can be customized with a wide range of fillings, making them versatile and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Ground beef, chicken, or pork
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salsa
  • Lettuce and tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook the seasoned meat in a skillet.
  2. Warm the tortillas on a stovetop or in a microwave.
  3. Prepare toppings like lettuce, salsa, and cheese.
  4. Fill the tortillas with cooked meat and toppings.
  5. Serve and enjoy with your favorite sauces.
  6. Squeeze some lime over the tacos for extra flavor!
